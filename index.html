Hi I'm Aysen Unlu. I am a Full Stack Web Developer. I have substantial technical skills and academic training in Computer Science.

I have a BS in Computer Engineering from Dokuz Eylul University (Turkey), an MS in Computer Engineering from Hacettepe University (Turkey) and an MS in Computer Science from Northeastern University (Boston,Massachusetts). I have recently attended the Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp and obtained a certificate from UCLA Extension School. I am a member of Upsilon Pi Epsilon and Golden Key Honor Societies.

I have extensive training and working knowledge of Java, C, C++, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, JQuery, Ajax, Node.js, Spring MVC, Thymeleaf, Hibernate, Struts, Maven, Git, Oracle, MySQL and Rest API's. I am a self-starter, ready to tackle large conceptual problems and have an attitute for learning. I have strong debugging and analytical skills and comfortable working in a dynamic, fast paced environment.

When I am not coding, I do volunteering in my daughter's school. I have been a treasurer, volunteer schedular as well as tutor in her class. I am an avid reader. I love baking with my daughter whenever I find extra time.
